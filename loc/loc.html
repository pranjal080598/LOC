GUIDELINE

ACM seeks to impose as few rules as possible in order to encourage maximum collaboration and innovation, but there are a few important things to make note of to ensure betterment.
1. Teams can be from one to four members.
2. The reporting time is sharp 8:30am on the 16th of March.
3. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner will be provided.
4. All participants are requested to get their respective Identity Cards.
5. All coders are requested to get their laptops. The solution must be developed during the event only.
